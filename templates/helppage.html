ISAS VIEWER : How to use

1 / 9
To visualize a dataset, choose the dataset, the variable, the timestamp and the depth values. Then click "PLOT".
2 / 9
You can adjust the color range if needed.
3 / 9
You can access a data value by selecting the feature tool and clicking on the position.
4 / 9
To plot a time serie for the dataset/variable/depth selection, you can draw the point (target icon) or fill the coordinates. Then click "Plot". Note : Time serie generation can take some time.
5 / 9
To plot a vertical profile for the dataset/variable/timestamp selection, you can draw the point (target icon) or fill the coordinates. Then click "Plot".
6 / 9
To plot a regionnal section for the dataset/variable/depth/timestamp selection. You can draw the area (selection icon) or fill the coordinates. You can also click the globe icon to use predefined areas. If a custom color range is used in the interactive plot, it is also used in the generated plot.
7 / 9
Color range can also be customized within the plot. Figure and/or data can also be downloaded using figure links.
8 / 9
To plot a vertical section for the dataset/variable/timestamp selection. You can draw the line (target icon, double click on the 2nd point to validate the line) or fill the coordinates. In the section plot, left will always be west. If a custom color range is used in the interactive plot, it is also used in the generated plot.
9 / 9
All tools can be applied in anomaly to the selected climatology.
